About Deryck Hodge

I'm a web developer working in media, entertainment, and publishing. I try to think deeply about how technology impacts creativity and then build software to enable storytellers to do their best work. I'm based in Atlanta, GA.

Professionally, I'm a senior engineering manager at Warner Bros. Discovery leading the Tooling, Infrastructure, and Performance teams within CNN Content Platforms. Previous stops include: Apple, Disney, AWS, Canonical, and the Washington Post, among others.

Full details on my professional career are available on my LinkedIn profile.

It's fun to say I’ve been figuring out how best to tell stories using the web since 1998. I went to school for English and imagined I would be a writer and a teacher, but then I discovered the web. I started trying to figure out how to share my writing online and then got into open source software development. I found Python and JavaScript and then Django, and I followed Django into building on the web for news sites. From there, my career took off.

I'm also a fan of media and entertainment outside of work. I love going to see movies in a theater, and I also have a nice home entertainment system. I'm a fan of comic books and reading fiction, especially literary fiction. I also love great music, games, sports, and travelling with my family.
